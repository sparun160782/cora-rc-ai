# ✅ 2. Detailed Functional Classification

## 🟢 A. AML / KYC / Financial Crime Control

### 📄 Documents:
- Master-Direction-Know-Your-Customer(KYC)Direction-2016-updated-August-14-2025
- 169MD

### 🎯 Core Purpose:
- Prevent Money Laundering (ML) and Terrorist Financing (TF)
- Mandate Customer Due Diligence (CDD) and monitoring

### 🔑 Key Themes:
- Customer identification (CIP, CDD)  
- Risk categorisation (low / medium / high)  
- Beneficial ownership identification  
- Transaction monitoring and suspicious reporting  
- FATF compliance  

👉 **These clearly fall under:**
- ✅ Regulatory Compliance → AML/KYC Domain

## 🟡 B. Credit Risk, Loan Transfer & Asset Lifecycle

### 📄 Document:
- RBI-DOR-2025-26-352_23122025162847657

### 🎯 Core Purpose:
- Define credit risk transfer mechanisms  
- Govern loan sales, securitisation, co-lending, stressed assets  

### 🔑 Key Themes:
- Loan transfer (assignment / participation)  
- Stressed asset transfer (NPA handling)  
- Co-lending arrangements  
- Risk sharing and capital treatment  

👉 **Classified as:**
- ✅ Credit Risk Management / Lending Lifecycle Regulation

## 🔵 C. NBFC Core Regulatory & Prudential Framework

### 📄 Document:
- RBI-MASTER-DIRECTION-NBFC-NSI-ND-29-08-23

### 🎯 Core Purpose:
- Regulate NBFC operations, capital, risk, and reporting  

### 🔑 Key Themes:
- Registration & applicability  
- Asset classification (Standard, NPA, etc.)  
- Capital adequacy & leverage  
- Provisioning rules  
- Lending practices and governance  

👉 **Classified as:**
- ✅ Institutional / Prudential Regulation (NBFC Supervision)
